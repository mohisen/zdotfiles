Why using anonymous links?

Webmasters can use this tool to prevent their site from appearing in the server logs of referred pages as referrer. The operators of the referred pages cannot see where their visitors come from.


To use our service:
http://knil.ws?url=http://google.com/ creates an anonymous link to google.com which prevents your site from appearing as a referrer in google.com's logfiles.
